Pasta Recipes

Herby Pasta

star star star star star by Olivia Pia
01

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until very al dente, about 2 minutes less than package directions.

02

Meanwhile, heat ¼ cup oil in a large Dutch oven or other heavy pot over medium. Add garlic and cook, stirring often and pressing down on cloves so they make good contact with the bottom of the pot, until golden and soft, about 5 minutes.

tips_and_updates

Add parsley and cook, stirring occasionally, until parsley releases some of its liquid.

03

Using tongs, transfer pasta to pot with sauce and add butter and ½ cup pasta cooking liquid. Cook, tossing and adding more pasta liquid as needed, until each strand of pasta is coated.

04

Remove pasta from heat and toss in basil, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Taste and season with salt as needed.
