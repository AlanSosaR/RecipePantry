Preparation Steps

1

Prepare the base

Mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter. Press firmly into the pan and chill for 30 minutes.

2

Cream Cheese Filling

Whisk cream cheese and icing sugar until smooth. Fold in 200g of cookie spread.

3

Assemble & Chill

Spread the mixture over the base. Smooth the top and refrigerate for 6 hours.
